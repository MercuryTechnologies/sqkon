# Concepts

Background on what Sqkon is, how it works under the hood, and where it sits in the
Kotlin storage landscape. Read these pages to decide whether Sqkon fits your use case
before you wire it into your app.

The pages in this section are short and skimmable — start with **What it is, what it
isn't** if you're evaluating, **Architecture** if you want to understand how reads and
writes flow through the library, and **Comparison** if you're weighing Sqkon against
Room, DataStore, Realm, MMKV, or raw SQLDelight.

- [What it is, what it isn't]({{ '/concepts/what-it-is/' | relative_url }}) — scope, fit, and stability commitments.
- [Architecture]({{ '/concepts/architecture/' | relative_url }}) — components, schema, and the lifecycle of an insert and a query.
- [Comparison]({{ '/concepts/comparison/' | relative_url }}) — Sqkon vs Room, DataStore, Realm, MMKV, and raw SQLDelight.
